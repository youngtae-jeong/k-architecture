Representative Architect

Kwon Eun-jung

K-Architectural Firm is a design firm dedicated to embodying the identity and excellence of Korean architecture. Our goal is to create innovative and high-quality buildings that reflect the harmony of tradition and modernity, aiming to promote Korean architecture to the world. Additionally, the 'K' in our name is derived from Architect Kwon Eun-jung's name, symbolizing her professionalism and trustworthiness. We strive to build strong relationships with our clients based on trust to deliver the best results. At K-Architectural Firm, we focus on enhancing the value of spaces through creative and practical designs. We are committed to sustainable architecture and providing customized solutions that meet our clients' needs. When the logo of K-Architects is rotated, the Korean word "집" (meaning "home") appears. This symbolizes the architect’s passion and aspiration for homes and architecture, and it carries a profound meaning that reflects the vision and dreams of the architect
